# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful and inclusive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Focus on technical issues, not individuals
- Welcome feedback and collaboration

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Repeated disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that violate this code of conduct.
